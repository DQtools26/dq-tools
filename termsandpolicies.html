Privacy Policy
Last updated · 2026
DQ Tools is designed to prioritize simplicity, transparency, and minimal data collection. Most tools work directly inside your browser and do not require an account.
Information We Collect
Most features on DQ Tools can be used without creating an account.
Optional Account Information — if you create an account, the following may be collected:
  • Name or nickname
  • Email address
  • Password — securely managed through Firebase Authentication. The site creator cannot view or access your password.
The only information visible to the site administrator is the account name and email address. Creating an account is completely optional.
How Your Data Is Used
Account data is only used to allow certain tools to save information across devices:
  • GPA Review — saves your GPA data
  • Course Planner — stores your planned courses
  • DQTasks — saves tasks, notes, calendar items, and preferences
Saved tool data is stored using Supabase. Data is linked to a user ID, not personal information.
Local Storage (When Not Logged In)
  • Some tools may store information locally in your browser
  • Local data may be removed if you clear your browser or switch devices
  • No local data is ever sent to external servers
Analytics
Basic analytics are used to track total visitors, page popularity, and general usage trends. No analytics are used for advertising or profiling.
Cookies
Limited cookies may be used for maintaining login sessions, remembering preferences, and supporting analytics.
Third-Party Services
GitHub Pages Firebase Authentication Supabase Google Fonts Google Analytics
Advertising
No ads, sponsorships, or affiliate links are used on DQ Tools. The site is completely free and ad-free.
Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated as new tools and features are added. Continued use of the site indicates acceptance of any updates.
